Course Overview

This comprehensive program is designed to develop well-rounded protocol officers equipped to serve in government, diplomatic, and corporate environments. Each week features a focused, interactive session combining theory, practical exercises, and real-world case studies. Participants gain mastery in diplomatic etiquette, cross-cultural awareness, VIP management, and crisis preparedness.

8 Weeks | Interactive Workshops | Capstone Project

Who Should Attend?

  • Protocol officers (current and aspiring)
  • Executive assistants and administrative professionals
  • Government and diplomatic staff
  • Event coordinators and managers
  • Foreign service officers and embassy personnel

✦ 8-Week Curriculum Structure ✦

Week 1

Foundations of Protocol

Introduction to protocol: definitions, history, and importance • The role of protocol in government, business, and diplomacy • Ethics and professional standards for protocol officers

Week 2

Communication & Etiquette

Verbal and non-verbal communication skills • Business and diplomatic etiquette • Writing official correspondence and invitations • Mastering the art of diplomatic language

Week 3

Cross-Cultural Awareness

Understanding cultural differences in protocol • Managing international guests and dignitaries • Cultural sensitivity and avoiding protocol blunders • Navigating diverse diplomatic traditions

Week 4

Event Planning & VIP Management

Planning official events and ceremonies • VIP and dignitary handling procedures • Seating arrangements, flag protocols, and order of precedence • Protocol for state banquets and official functions

Week 5

Risk Assessment & Crisis Preparedness

Identifying and assessing protocol-related risks • Developing crisis prevention strategies • Building a crisis-ready protocol team • Threat assessment for high-profile events

Week 6

Emergency Response & Crisis Management

Creating emergency response plans for protocol events • Roles and responsibilities during emergencies • Crisis communication and coordination with authorities • Post-incident analysis and reporting

Week 7

Professional Image & Representation

Personal branding and professional image • Phone, email, and digital etiquette • Representing your organisation at high-level events • Building executive presence and gravitas

Week 8

Capstone Project & Assessment

Development and presentation of a protocol/event management plan • Case study analysis and group discussion • Final assessment and feedback • Course review and certification ceremony

Each session blends theoretical foundations with hands-on application. Participants engage in:

Capstone Project

Participants develop and present a comprehensive protocol/event management plan addressing a real-world government or diplomatic scenario, demonstrating mastery of all eight modules.
