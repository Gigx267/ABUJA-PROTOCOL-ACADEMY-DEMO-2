✦ 4-Day Workshop Curriculum ✦

Day 1

Hospitality Protocol Foundations

Importance of protocol in hospitality • Etiquette for international and local guests • Cultural sensitivity and first impressions • Understanding guest psychology and expectations • The art of welcome and farewell rituals.

Day 2

Elite Guest Management

VIP and dignitary handling protocols • Greeting, seating, and precedence (order of importance) • Managing guest expectations with discretion • Security considerations for high-profile visitors • Personalized service strategies.

Day 3

Event & Reception Excellence

Organizing formal and informal events • Table settings, service styles, and silver service techniques • Managing guest lists, RSVPs, and seating arrangements • Coordination with culinary and banquet teams • Protocol for toasts, speeches, and cultural nuances.

Day 4

Concierge Mastery & Practical Simulation

The art of concierge service • Handling special requests with creativity and efficiency • Problem solving for guest satisfaction • Real-life scenario role-plays • Group feedback and improvement session • Capstone practical assessment.
