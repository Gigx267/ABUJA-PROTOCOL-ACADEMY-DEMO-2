✦ AVIATION EXCELLENCE ✦

Cabin Crew & Air Hostess Workshop

An intensive, practical program introducing aspiring aviation professionals to the core skills, safety protocols, and world-class service standards required for a successful career in the skies. Designed with leading aviation benchmarks and Nigerian regulatory context.

✦ DOWNLOAD PROGRAM BROCHURE ✦

Who Should Attend

  • Aspiring air hostesses & cabin crew
  • Customer service professionals seeking aviation roles
  • Young graduates & career changers interested in travel industry
  • Hospitality professionals aiming for in-flight excellence

Workshop Format

Duration: 4 days intensive (32 hours total) — practical & classroom mix

Format: In-person at Abuja Protocol Academy HQ, with full mock-up cabin sessions, evacuation drills, and airport familiarization.

Next cohort: contact admissions for dates

Key Learning Areas

  • Aviation & Cabin Crew Roles: Industry overview, professional responsibilities, airline operations
  • Safety & Emergency Procedures: Aircraft familiarization, door operations, evacuation slide, fire & smoke training, ditching/sea drills
  • First Aid & Aviation Medicine: Basic life support, medical emergencies in-flight
  • Passenger Service & Customer Care: In-flight procedures, conflict resolution, cultural sensitivity
  • Crew Resource Management: Teamwork, leadership, pilot incapacitation protocols
  • Professional Image & Grooming: Health, well-being, presentation, confidence building
  • Regulatory Compliance: NCAA regulations, dangerous goods, security protocols

Practical Sessions

  • Mock fire-fighting & evacuation drills (realistic cabin mock-up)
  • Crowd control & emergency leadership
  • Passenger safety demonstrations & brace positions
  • Airport/aircraft familiarization tour
  • Service role-play: handling VIPs and difficult passengers
Evacuation drills CRM simulation Grooming standards NCAA compliance

Entry Requirements

  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • O’Level (WAEC/NECO) or higher qualification
  • Good health, professional grooming & basic communication skills
  • Passion for service & aviation excellence

Certification & Recognition

Graduates receive a Certificate of Completion from Abuja Protocol Academy, meeting industry-recognized standards and preparing participants for airline recruitment processes and NCAA licensing pathways.

Hands-on practical training for immediate employability

Career Launch Benefits

Preparation for airline recruitment, networking with aviation professionals, access to alumni community, and enhanced soft skills for global aviation roles.

✦ COMPREHENSIVE CURRICULUM ✦

Day 1: Aviation Foundation

Industry intro, roles & responsibilities, professional ethics, airline branding, uniform standards, introduction to NCAA regulations.

Day 2: Safety & Emergency

Aircraft doors & exits, evacuation drills, fire & smoke simulation, ditching procedures, first aid/CPR certification basics.

Day 3: Service Excellence

In-flight service procedures, cultural sensitivity, handling difficult passengers, conflict resolution, fine dining etiquette at altitude.

Day 4: Crew Resource & Assessment

CRM teamwork, pilot incapacitation drills, grooming & final assessment, interview preparation & airline recruitment insights.

World-class Training Environment

Our workshop integrates state-of-the-art cabin mock-ups, real aviation equipment, and experienced facilitators who have served in international airlines and Nigerian aviation authorities. You'll experience real-world emergency scenarios and passenger service simulations that mirror airline assessments.

Networking & Placement Support

Participants gain exclusive access to recruitment agencies, airline career fairs, and professional mentorship. Alumni have been placed with domestic and international carriers due to APA's strong industry ties.
