How to Apply

Join Nigeria’s premier institution for protocol, diplomacy, and professional excellence. Our streamlined admissions process ensures you can focus on what matters — building the skills to lead with confidence, grace, and global perspective.

✦ APPLICATION STEPS ✦

How to Apply

1

Step 1: Explore Programmes

Review our available courses to identify the programme that best suits your career goals. From Government Solutions to Youth Protocol, find your path to excellence.

2

Step 2: Prepare Necessary Documents

Ensure all required documents are ready for a smooth application process. Having your credentials organised accelerates your admission.

3

Step 3: Select Your Desired Course

Choose your preferred course and confirm your interest in the programme. Our admissions team is available to guide you if you need clarification.

4

Step 4: Complete Fee Payment

Proceed with payment to secure your enrollment into the selected programme. Once payment is confirmed, your seat is reserved and you receive onboarding details.

For corporate or group enrollments, please contact our partnerships desk for a tailored registration experience.

✦ ADMISSIONS JOURNEY ✦

Admissions Process

1

Step 1: Explore Our Programmes

Discover our range of courses and select a track aligned with your aspirations. Whether you're an executive, a young leader, or a hospitality professional — we have a programme for you.

2

Step 2: Prepare Required Documents

Get all necessary documents ready for submission. Typical requirements include academic credentials, identification, and professional references (if applicable).

3

Step 3: Select Your Preferred Course

Indicate your chosen programme and confirm your enrollment interest. Our team will reach out to verify details and answer any final questions.

4

Step 4: Secure Your Admission

Complete the required fee payment to finalize your admission. Upon completion, you will receive a welcome package, schedule, and access to preparatory resources.

Scholarships and early-bird discounts available for select cohorts — inquire with our admissions office.

Required Documents (Checklist)

To ensure a seamless application, have these items ready before you begin the enrollment process.

Completed Application Form Government-issued ID / Passport Academic Certificates / Transcripts Professional CV / Resume (for executive tracks) Passport-sized Photograph Letter of Motivation (optional but recommended)

*Specific programmes may require additional credentials — our admissions counsellor will inform you after initial inquiry.
